# Proyecto BlackBox

**Breaking the Wall of the Missing Minutes**

Proyecto BlackBox is a non-profit initiative that turns everyday smartphones into personal forensic black boxes — systems that preserve and securely share critical digital evidence during the first, most decisive minutes of a disappearance.

Inspired by the black box in an aircraft, the idea is simple: don't wait for an investigation to begin before the evidence starts disappearing. Preserve it the moment it's still there, and deliver it — sealed, encrypted, and verifiable — directly to the families and investigators who need it most.

This is not another panic-button safety app. It's a new category of mobile digital forensics, built for one purpose: giving families answers, faster.

## What it captures
- GPS location and movement patterns
- Wi-Fi and Bluetooth proximity signals
- Gyroscope and motion data
- Cryptographically sealed timestamps
- Encrypted delivery to trusted contacts chosen in advance

## Status
Early-stage concept, submitted to Falling Walls (Computer Science / Mobile Digital Forensics).

## Non-profit
Proyecto BlackBox is built for families, funded by donors and volunteers — never sold, never monetized.
